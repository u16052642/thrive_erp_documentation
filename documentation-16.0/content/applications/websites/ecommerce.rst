=========
eCommerce
=========

Run a modern open-source online store with Thrive Bureau ERP eCommerce. Learn how to sell online, promote
products and increase your average cart sizes.

.. important::
   Thrive Bureau ERP offers a :ref:`free custom domain name <domain-name/Thrive Bureau ERP-register>` to all Thrive Bureau ERP Online
   databases for one year. Visitors can then access your website with an address such as
   ``www.example.com`` rather than the default ``example.Thrive Bureau ERP.com``.
